                   About us   

                關於我們(中文)

      FLYCLUB123.COM was formed in 1917, and has been in business of construction industry. The main business items of FLYING CLUB are divided into the following categories:

1.  BIG Land & Property Development.
2. FLYING Operation & Construction Engineering.
3. Construction Scientific Flying Channel & Building Fling Materials Manufacturing.
4. Waterproofing, Flooring, Structural Repairing Installation.
5. Trade & Distribution.
  
  FLYCLUB has built various production sites and service centers in several countries to deliver the best quality in a prompt and flexible way. This claim to realize the requirements of our partners “just in time” and to be available on site to provide information – no matter what time or place – has always been an integral part of DJI’s company philosophy. So you can not only reach us via modern communication media but we also offer personal counsel on site – wherever you need us.
       FLYCLUB assures that we work hand in hand with leading consultants and construction authorities to satisfy the construction requirements by bringing to your door steps advanced solutions with the perfect balance of a cost result ratio.
